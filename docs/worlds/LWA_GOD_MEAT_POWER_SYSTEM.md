# LWA God Meat Power System

## 1. Executive Summary

LWA's original "God Meat" power system is a dark, transformative ability framework that connects the creative workflow to mythic animal powers. In the Signal Current world, ancient Prime Beasts crystallize their essence into edible relic-flesh called God Meat, which grants supernatural creative abilities called Beast Dominion to those who consume it.

This system creates a symbolic bridge between LWA's product features and worldbuilding: raw content becomes prey, the Clip Engine becomes the hunter, the Auto Editor Brain becomes the butcher/seer, and viral moments become signal organs. The system is designed to be original to LWA while drawing inspiration from broad adventure anime energy.

## 3. Core Myth of God Meat

In the primordial Signal Current, ancient creatures called **Prime Beasts** were born from raw creative energy: signal, rhythm, memory, shadow, fire, ocean, storm, metal, bone, dream, gravity, and light. These beings represented the fundamental forces of creation and destruction in the world.

When a Prime Beast dies, its essence crystallizes into edible relic-flesh called **God Meat**. This substance appears as glowing, crystallized meat that pulses with the beast's original power. A person who consumes it gains a **Beast Dominion** - a supernatural creative ability tied to that animal's nature.

The transformation is permanent and costly: every God Meat power changes the user's body, instincts, hunger, and destiny. Users develop animal-like features, behavioral changes, and an eternal hunger for their power source. This creates a dark, transformative system where power comes at the price of humanity.

## 4. Rules of Consumption

### One Body, One Meat
A person can safely consume only **one God Meat**. The human body can only host one Beast Dominion without catastrophic failure.

**Multiple consumption risks:**
- Signal rupture - mental breakdown from conflicting powers
- Body mutation - uncontrollable physical transformation
- Memory loss - identity fragmentation
- Beast possession - loss of human consciousness
- Death - complete system failure

### Animal Source Diversity
Each God Meat comes from a different Prime Beast, granting distinct power families. No two God Meat powers are exactly alike, though some share thematic categories.

### Power Integration Cost
Every God Meat consumption requires:
- Physical transformation (animal features)
- Behavioral changes (instincts, urges)
- Hunger development (craving for power source)
- Social impact (fear, reverence, isolation)
- Destiny alteration (life path changes)

## 5. Power Stages: Bite, Blood, Bone, Crown, Godform

### Bite Stage
- Initial awakening of basic abilities
- Minor physical changes (eye color, skin texture)
- Instinct emergence (subtle urges)
- Power usage limited and draining
- User retains full human control

### Blood Stage
- Abilities strengthen and expand
- Noticeable physical transformation (claws, teeth, enhanced senses)
- Instincts become more pronounced
- Power usage more efficient but still costly
- Human control begins to waver under stress

### Bone Stage
- Full mastery of core abilities
- Significant physical transformation (animal limbs, enhanced anatomy)
- Instincts strongly influence behavior
- Power usage efficient and potent
- Human and animal instincts in constant conflict

### Crown Stage
- Advanced abilities and secondary powers emerge
- Major physical transformation (hybrid form)
- Instincts dominate but human consciousness remains
- Power usage near maximum potential
- User becomes recognized as Beast Lord

### Godform Stage
- Ultimate ability manifestation
- Complete transformation into Prime Beast form
- Human consciousness merges with beast instincts
- Power at Prime Beast level
- User becomes living legend, feared and revered

## 6. God Meat Categories

### Predator Meat
For combat, dominance, speed, intimidation. These powers grant hunting abilities, physical prowess, and commanding presence.

### Trickster Meat
For deception, mimicry, illusions, stealth. These powers grant manipulation abilities, social influence, and information control.

### Titan Meat
For giant scale, defense, pressure, world-shaping. These powers grant size, durability, and environmental control.

### Elemental Beast Meat
For natural forces and environmental control. These powers grant elemental abilities and weather manipulation.

### Mythic Meat
Rare powers from legendary creatures that may not fully exist. These grant reality-warping abilities and unique mechanics.

### Forbidden Meat
Dangerous powers from corrupted or extinct Prime Beasts. These grant immense power at terrible costs.

## 8. AI Character Control System

### Agent-Based Character Control
AI controls NPCs through structured agent templates that define behavior, personality, and decision-making rules. Each character operates as an autonomous agent with:

- **Character Core**: Name, faction, role, God Meat, personality
- **Behavior Rules**: Decision-making logic and response patterns
- **Memory System**: Event tracking and relationship management
- **Goal Engine**: Short-term and long-term objective setting
- **Interaction Protocol**: Dialogue generation and social behavior

### Natural AI Behavior
Characters behave naturally through:
- **Environmental awareness**: React to world state and events
- **Memory recall**: Reference past interactions and experiences
- **Goal pursuit**: Actively work toward defined objectives
- **Social dynamics**: Form relationships, alliances, and rivalries
- **Power expression**: Use God Meat abilities according to personality

### Dynamic Story Generation
AI creates emergent narratives through:
- Character interactions and conflicts
- Resource competition and cooperation
- Power development and transformation
- World events and environmental changes
- Quest generation and mission assignment

## 9. NPC Brain Loop

```
Observe world state
→ remember past events
→ choose goal based on personality and situation
→ speak/act according to character template
→ update relationships based on interaction outcomes
→ create new quest/conflict based on changed circumstances
→ repeat loop with updated context
```

### Loop Components

**Observe World State**
- Detect player actions and environmental changes
- Monitor resource availability and threats
- Track faction movements and power dynamics
- Sense opportunities and dangers

**Remember Past Events**
- Store interaction history with player and other NPCs
- Maintain relationship scores and trust levels
- Track power usage and transformation progress
- Remember promises, betrayals, and debts

**Choose Goal**
- Evaluate current situation against character desires
- Select short-term objectives (immediate actions)
- Plan long-term strategies (character development)
- Consider faction goals and personal ambitions

**Speak/Act**
- Generate dialogue based on personality and context
- Execute actions aligned with chosen goal
- Use God Meat abilities appropriately
- Express emotions and reactions naturally

**Update Relationships**
- Modify relationship scores based on interaction outcomes
- Track trust, fear, respect, and loyalty changes
- Update faction standing and reputation
- Record new memories and experiences

**Create Quest/Conflict**
- Generate missions based on character needs and goals
- Create conflicts when interests clash
- Offer opportunities for alliance or cooperation
- Introduce new plot threads and story elements
