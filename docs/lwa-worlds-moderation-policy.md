# LWA Worlds Moderation Policy Draft

## Purpose

Protect creators, buyers, UGC assets, marketplace campaigns, and the LWA brand from unsafe, stolen, misleading, or illegal content.

## Review Targets

- marketplace campaigns
- campaign submissions
- UGC assets
- template products
- profile names
- avatars
- titles and descriptions
- uploaded files
- rights claims
- fraud flags

## Moderation States

- pending
- approved
- rejected
- flagged
- escalated
- removed
- appealed

## Human Review Required

- public UGC listing
- marketplace sale approval
- payout hold or release
- rights claim resolution
- fraud escalation
- account ban
- disputed campaign resolution

## User-Facing Principles

- Users must own or have rights to submitted content.
- LWA may reject, remove, or hold content during review.
- LWA may hold payouts during disputes, rights claims, or fraud review.
- Users may appeal moderation decisions through support.

## Not Legal Advice

This document is an operational moderation draft and is not legal advice.
