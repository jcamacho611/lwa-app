# IWA Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

IWA is an AI content repurposing service that helps users generate clips, hooks, captions, and related metadata from video URLs.

## What We Collect

- Video URLs submitted by the user
- Generated clip metadata such as timestamps, titles, hooks, captions, and processing status
- Basic operational logs needed to keep the service running and debug failures

## What We Do Not Intentionally Collect

- Payment card information inside the iOS app
- Contact lists
- Photos, camera, or microphone data from the device

## How We Use Data

- To process submitted video URLs
- To generate clips and related output
- To maintain and improve service reliability
- To troubleshoot failures and support customer requests

## Third-Party Services

IWA may use third-party infrastructure and AI providers to process requests, host the backend, and generate outputs.

## Data Retention

Generated assets and operational logs may be retained for debugging, product improvement, or customer support purposes unless deleted earlier as part of normal service cleanup.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect service data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

## Children

IWA is not directed to children under 13.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the IWA team through your official support channel.
