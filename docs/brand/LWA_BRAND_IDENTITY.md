# LWA Brand Identity

## Overview

LWA operates with a dual-brand identity strategy that balances premium mythic branding with focused professional functionality.

## Brand Architecture

### LWA (Technical/Product Brand)
**Purpose**: Professional video clipping and generation platform
**Usage**: 
- App name and technical references
- Backend/frontend systems
- Company and repository naming
- Product documentation
- Developer and user interfaces

**Characteristics**:
- Clean, functional, creator-native
- Professional and reliable
- Performance-focused
- Tool-centric

### LEE-WUH (Mascot/Cultural Brand)
**Purpose**: "The Last Creator" - Brand character and cultural identity
**Usage**:
- Mascot and brand moments
- Merchandise and cultural products
- Hero character and storytelling
- Social media and marketing
- Community engagement

**Characteristics**:
- Cute but dangerous anime final-boss chibi
- African + Japanese fusion aesthetics
- American streetwear influence
- Gold/Purple/Black luxury palette
- Big jeweled dreads
- Overlord/creator-god aura
- Furry creature energy

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Gold (luxury, premium)
- **Secondary**: Purple (creativity, mystique)  
- **Tertiary**: Black (sophistication, power)
- **Accent**: White (clarity, contrast)

### Typography
- **LWA**: Clean, modern, technical
- **LEE-WUH**: Stylized, expressive, character-driven

### Mascot Design
- **Style**: Anime chibi with final boss energy
- **Features**: Large jeweled dreads, expressive eyes
- **Attire**: Streetwear meets traditional elements
- **Aura**: Powerful yet approachable creator energy

## Brand Application Guidelines

### Product Interface (LWA)
- Keep mascot at edges, not blocking core functionality
- Use clean, professional design for main tools
- Maintain creator-native aesthetic
- Focus on performance and reliability

### Brand Moments (LEE-WUH)
- Homepage hero character
- Loading and processing states
- Empty states and onboarding
- Social media announcements
- Merchandise and collectibles
- Community content and tips

### Brand Rules

1. **Technical Context**: Use "LWA"
   - App interfaces
   - Backend systems
   - Documentation
   - Developer tools

2. **Cultural Context**: Use "LEE-WUH"
   - Mascot appearances
   - Merchandise
   - Social posts
   - Brand storytelling

3. **Mixed Context**: Use both strategically
   - Homepage: "LWA powered by LEE-WUH"
   - Loading: "LEE-WUH is working..."
   - Success: "LEE-WUH approves!"
