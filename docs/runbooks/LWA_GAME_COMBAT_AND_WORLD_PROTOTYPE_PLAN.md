# LWA Game Combat and World Prototype Plan

## Overview

This runbook documents the future-only game combat and world prototype plan for LWA, covering Seven Agents roles, God Meat progression stages, Prime Beast encounters, realm traversal, combat mechanics, VFX systems, NPC AI, and multiplayer boundaries. All gameplay systems are designated as FUTURE ONLY and require prototype gates before any implementation.

## Seven Agents World Roles (FUTURE ONLY)

### Agent Archetypes
```python
Seven Agent Roles:
1. Signal Agent: Realm navigation and communication
2. Current Agent: Energy manipulation and flow control
3. Onyx Agent: Shadow manipulation and stealth
4. Gold Agent: Light amplification and protection
5. Nova Agent: Explosive energy and burst damage
6. Mirror Agent: Reflection and illusion tactics
7. Hook Agent: Grappling and terrain manipulation
```

### Role Progression System
- **Tier 1**: Basic ability mastery
- **Tier 2**: Advanced technique combinations
- **Tier 3**: Realm-specific specialization
- **Tier 4**: Multi-agent coordination
- **Tier 5**: God Meat stage adaptation

### Agent Synergy Matrix
```python
Synergy Combinations:
- Signal + Current: Enhanced realm traversal
- Onyx + Gold: Light/shadow balance attacks
- Nova + Mirror: Explosive reflection chains
- Hook + Signal: Long-range coordination
- All Seven: God Meat stage unlocking
```

## God Meat Stages (FUTURE ONLY)

### Stage 1: Bite
- **Description**: Initial God Meat contact and basic corruption
- **Mechanics**: First encounter, basic resistance testing
- **Rewards**: Basic relic fragments, initial power boost
- **Risks**: Temporary stat reduction, corruption exposure

### Stage 2: Blood
- **Description**: Blood flow manipulation and life force extraction
- **Mechanics**: Health drain mechanics, blood magic abilities
- **Rewards**: Enhanced regeneration, blood-based attacks
- **Risks**: Permanent health reduction, corruption acceleration

### Stage 3: Bone
- **Description**: Skeletal transformation and structural reinforcement
- **Mechanics**: Bone armor, skeletal summons, structural attacks
- **Rewards**: Physical damage resistance, bone weapon creation
- **Risks**: Mobility reduction, appearance transformation

### Stage 4: Crown
- **Description**: Mental domination and realm control abilities
- **Mechanics**: Mind control, realm manipulation, psychic attacks
- **Rewards**: Realm influence, psychic powers, control abilities
- **Risks**: Loss of free will, personality fragmentation

### Stage 5: Godform
- **Description**: Complete God Meat integration and transcendence
- **Mechanics**: God-like abilities, realm creation, ultimate power
- **Rewards**: Transcendence, realm creation, ultimate abilities
- **Risks**: Complete loss of humanity, permanent transformation

### God Meat Progression Rules
```python
Progression Requirements:
- Stage Unlocks: Complete previous stage challenges
- Corruption Resistance: Must maintain mental stability
- Agent Coordination: Requires multiple agent cooperation
- Realm Conditions: Specific realm environmental factors
- Sacrifice Costs: Permanent stat/skill reductions
```

## VFX Families (FUTURE ONLY)

### Gold Current Trails
- **Visual Style**: Flowing golden energy streams
- **Usage**: Agent movement, energy attacks, realm effects
- **Interactions**: Illuminates dark areas, powers up agents
- **Technical Requirements**: Particle systems, flow simulation
- **Performance Impact**: Medium GPU load, optimized for mid-range

### Onyx Pressure Fog
- **Visual Style**: Dense shadow fog with pressure distortion
- **Usage**: Stealth mechanics, area denial, atmosphere
- **Interactions**: Reduces visibility, dampens sound effects
- **Technical Requirements**: Volumetric fog, distortion effects
- **Performance Impact**: High GPU load, LOD optimization needed

### Signal Fracture Sparks
- **Visual Style**: Digital data sparks and glitch effects
- **Usage**: Communication abilities, data manipulation
- **Interactions**: Reveals hidden information, disrupts systems
- **Technical Requirements**: Shader effects, particle systems
- **Performance Impact**: Low to medium GPU load

### God Meat Pulse
- **Visual Style**: Corrupted energy pulses with organic movement
- **Usage**: Corruption mechanics, power boosts, transformation
- **Interactions**: Spreads corruption, enhances abilities
- **Technical Requirements**: Complex particle systems, distortion
- **Performance Impact**: High GPU load, optimization critical

### Beast Crown Flare
- **Visual Style**: Crown-shaped energy burst with royal aesthetics
- **Usage**: Beast abilities, power attacks, area effects
- **Interactions**: Stuns enemies, powers up allies
- **Technical Requirements**: Complex geometry, lighting effects
- **Performance Impact**: Medium to high GPU load

### Hookstorm Static
- **Visual Style**: Metallic static with hook-like shapes
- **Usage**: Grappling mechanics, terrain manipulation
- **Interactions**: Pulls objects, creates anchor points
- **Technical Requirements**: Physics simulation, particle effects
- **Performance Impact**: Medium GPU and CPU load

### Relic Wake
- **Visual Style**: Trailing energy from activated relics
- **Usage**: Relic abilities, power trails, status effects
- **Interactions**: Leaves persistent effects, powers up areas
- **Technical Requirements**: Trail systems, glow effects
- **Performance Impact**: Low to medium GPU load

## Co-op / UDP Multiplayer Boundaries (FUTURE ONLY)

### Multiplayer Architecture
```python
Network Layers:
1. Local Co-op: Same device multiplayer (split-screen)
2. LAN Multiplayer: Local network play
3. Online Co-op: Internet-based multiplayer
4. Cross-Platform: Different device compatibility
5. Cloud Sync: Progress and world state synchronization
```

### Player Interaction Systems
- **Shared World**: All players in same realm instance
- **Party System**: Group formation and management
- **Trade System**: Item and resource exchange
- **Communication**: Voice and text chat options
- **Progress Sharing**: Shared quest progress and achievements

### Technical Boundaries
- **Player Limit**: Maximum 4 players per session
- **Bandwidth Requirements**: Minimum 10 Mbps upload/download
- **Latency Tolerance**: Maximum 200ms acceptable latency
- **Region Locking**: Players limited to same region servers
- **Platform Compatibility**: Cross-play limited by platform policies

### Synchronization Challenges
- **World State**: Realm conditions must sync across clients
- **NPC Behavior**: AI actions synchronized between players
- **Combat Events**: Damage and effects must be consistent
- **Inventory Management**: Item ownership and trading
- **Quest Progress**: Shared advancement tracking

### Security Considerations
- **Anti-Cheat**: Prevent unauthorized modifications
- **Data Validation**: Server-side verification of client actions
- **Rate Limiting**: Prevent spam and exploitation
- **Privacy Protection**: Secure player data transmission
- **Content Filtering**: Prevent inappropriate content sharing
