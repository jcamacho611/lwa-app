## Description

IWA helps creators turn long videos into short-form content faster.

Paste a video link, find the strongest moments, and turn long-form content into clips, hooks, captions, and timestamps built for short-form posting.

Whether you are repurposing interviews, podcasts, tutorials, talking-head videos, or creator content, IWA helps you stop wasting time digging through footage and start pulling usable moments fast.

What you can do with IWA:

- Generate short-form clip ideas from long videos
- Find strong moments faster
- Create hooks and captions for posting
- Pull timestamps for easier editing
- Repurpose content into a faster publishing workflow

Built for creators who want more output from the content they already made.

## Review Notes

IWA is an AI-powered content repurposing app for turning long-form videos into short-form content assets.

To test the app:

1. Open the app
2. Paste a supported public video link
3. Tap the generate button
4. Review the returned clips, hooks, captions, and timestamps

This build does not sell digital purchases inside the iOS app. Mobile users manage support and policy information from the Settings screen.
