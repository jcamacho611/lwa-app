# LWA Worlds Content Rights Policy Draft

## Purpose

Make rights ownership, disputes, and takedown-style review visible before UGC and marketplace content scale.

## Claim States

- open
- in_review
- information_requested
- resolved
- rejected
- escalated

## Required User Assertions

- UGC assets require rights confirmation.
- Campaign submissions require rights confirmation.
- Uploads that become source, render, or UGC assets must require rights confirmation.

## Review Principles

- A user checkbox is an assertion, not proof.
- Human review remains required for rights disputes.
- LWA should not publish disputed UGC while a claim is unresolved.
- LWA should not release payout placeholders when an open rights claim blocks the underlying asset.

## Not Legal Advice

This document is an operational draft and must be reviewed before production enforcement.
