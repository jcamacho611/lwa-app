# LWA Worlds Integrations

## AI

### OpenAI

Use for hooks, captions, titles, scoring explanation, structured output, marketplace campaign briefs, and quest drafts.

Env:

- `OPENAI_API_KEY`

### Anthropic

Use for long transcript analysis, creator strategy, world bible consistency, and dispute summaries.

Env:

- `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY`

## Social

### YouTube

Use for video metadata, channel metadata, playlist metadata, and future publishing after OAuth/app review.

Env:

- `YOUTUBE_API_KEY`
- `GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID`
- `GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET`

### TikTok

Use later for login, sharing/export, and content posting only where approved.

Env:

- `TIKTOK_CLIENT_KEY`
- `TIKTOK_CLIENT_SECRET`

### Instagram

Use later for creator/business publishing and insights.

Env:

- `META_APP_ID`
- `META_APP_SECRET`

### Twitch

Use for channel lookup, VOD metadata, and streamer campaigns.

Env:

- `TWITCH_CLIENT_ID`
- `TWITCH_CLIENT_SECRET`

### Polymarket

Use for trend intelligence only. Do not use for betting, trading, gambling, or user transactions.

## Payments

### Whop

Use for memberships, founder pass access, subscriptions, and gated products.

Env:

- `WHOP_API_KEY`
- `WHOP_WEBHOOK_SECRET`

### Stripe Connect

Use later for marketplace payments, seller payouts, and platform fees.

Env:

- `STRIPE_SECRET_KEY`
- `STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET`
- `STRIPE_CONNECT_CLIENT_ID`

## Blockchain

### Wallet / Future LWA Chain

Use later for wallet connection, proof badges, collectibles, and creator proof records.

Do not use for token launch in MVP, crypto payouts, staking, yield, or gambling.
