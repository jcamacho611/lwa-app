# LWA Sales Truth Language

## Purpose
This document gives the company claim-safe, founder-aligned language for sales, demos, landing pages, and outreach.

## Core Sales Phrase

> Give LWA the source. It builds the content package.

## Expanded Version

> LWA is an any-source AI content engine for creators, clippers, agencies, and streamers. Give it video, audio, music, a prompt, a Twitch or stream source, or a campaign objective, and it helps turn that source into ranked creator-ready packages with hooks, captions, visuals, post order, scores, and rendered assets when available.

## Allowed Language

- any source in
- creator-ready content out
- ranked clip packages
- rendered when available
- strategy-only fallback
- hooks, captions, timestamps
- helps prepare clips
- helps speed up repurposing
- recommended post order
- manual campaign preparation

## Forbidden Language

- guaranteed viral
- guaranteed views
- guaranteed revenue
- guaranteed payout
- guaranteed income
- auto-posts
- submits campaigns automatically
- private-video bypass
- replaces editors completely

## Monetization Truth

Whop is one option, not the whole business.

Use language like:

- upgrade on Whop
- request custom clip pack
- support the build
- book demo
- join creator / referral program

## Founder Truth

Do not reduce the product to:

- “paste a YouTube link”
- “Whop clipping tool”
- “basic viral clipper”

Keep it aligned to:

- any-source engine
- creator workflow system
- packaging and repurposing engine
